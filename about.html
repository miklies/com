Graphic designer and creative manager working at the intersection of visual identity and digital experience.

Based in Seoul, I work fluently across English and Korean to serve clients and teams in both cultural contexts. My practice spans brand identity, UI/UX systems, and typographic direction — grounded in the belief that good design is invisible when it's working.

I've led creative teams and cross-functional projects, with a particular eye for the details that separate considered work from generic output. Typography, hierarchy, and restraint are the tools I reach for first.

When I'm not designing, I'm thinking about type classifications, Korean design identity, and the next World Cup qualifier.
